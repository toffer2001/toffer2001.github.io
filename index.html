About Me

As time is our most valuable commodity, I enjoy spending precious and unforgettable moments with my family. So although I thoroughly enjoy learning a new skill to code, family is my first focus, my pride, and my joy. I've been married for 7 years, and my wife McKenzie and I have 2.5 kids. Taiden is 4 years old, Declan is 2 years old, and in November we will celebrate our first baby girl!

During the week, I dedicate much of my day working for a medical device company called Larada Sciences. We offer an FDA cleared medical device that is a solution to kill human head lice. It is very rewarding to be part of a service that helps so many kids and parents. In almost my 5 years with the company, I have been privileged to see our network of clinics grow to 175+ in the United States and 100+ clinics internationally. If you would like to learn more, check out Lice Clinics of America.
