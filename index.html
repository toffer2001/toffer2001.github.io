About Me

Time is our most valuable commodity. Therefore, I enjoy spending precious, unforgettable moments with my family every day. Although in the evenings I've recently engaged in learning a new skill to code, family is my first focus; they are my pride, my joy, and my motivation to radiate as a a positive influence in this world.

My wife, McKenzie, and I have been married for 7 years. We have 2.5 kids. Taiden is four years old, Declan is two, and in November we will celebrate our first baby girl!

During the week, I dedicate much of my day with a medical device company called Larada Sciences. Our flagship product is an FDA cleared medical device that is a solution to kill human head lice. It is very rewarding to be part of a service that helps kids and families. In almost my 5 years with the company, I have been privileged to help build our network of clinics to 175+ in the United States and 100+ clinics internationally. If you would like to learn more, check out Lice Clinics of America.

Lastly, in addition to my late nights siting and coding on a computer, I enjoy staying physically active with my hobby (more like a passion) of ballroom dance. I was fortunate to participate on the BYU Ballroom Dance Team, and today I teach private lessons for new and advanced dancers as well as instruct youth technique classes at Davis County Ballroom.
